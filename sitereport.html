Reflective Discussion

Over the course of this module, I learned the basics of web development, including HTML structure, CSS styling, as well as responsive design techniques. The development of this portfolio taught me how to organize the multi-page website, maintain consistent navigation, and also apply design principles such as color schemes, typography, and also the layout grids. I also encountered challenges in debugging CSS alignment issues and ensuirng that the site looked good on desktop as well as mobile devices as well. Through trial and error, I learned the importance of HTML on how it is used and is used for developing websites in day to day lives. I even grasped the knowledge of it as well.

Design Decisions

  • Font choice: Clean, sans-serif for readability
  • Color scheme: Light background with contrasting text.
  • Layout: Consistent grid layout for Projects and resposive design.
  • Navigation: Simple top navigation bar linking all pages for ease of access.
